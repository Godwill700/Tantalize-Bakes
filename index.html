Holiday Cakes

A choice of decadent moist cakes you can gift your loved ones during the holidays or any time really.

Death by chocolate

The cake of your dreams. Three gorgeous layers of chocolate cake with a chocolate milk soak, chocolate cookie crumbs, creamy chocolate buttercream.

Strawberry Vanilla

A fluffy vanilla cake loaded with fresh sweet strawberries and a rich cream cheese whipped cream. The perfect summer recipe for any celebration or get together..

Fresh Mint

Fresh Mint is a rich dark chocolate cake filled and topped with fresh mint buttercream. Add a little fresh mint and chopped chocolate to the buttercream and take it to a whole new minty level!

Rose Plum Wedding cake

Indulge in this decadent Rose Plum fruit cake. It is made with stunning plum truffle sugar paste roses with mauve hydrangea.

Welcome to Tantalize Bakes

...A taste of the future

We are a bakery dedicated to creating products with an uncompromised taste

Tantalize Bakes is a bakery in Harare, Zimbabwe. We make custom cakes and cupcakes with the finest ingredients and we hand decorate them with the utmost attention to detail. We’re seriously passionate about cakes and cupcakes and bringing joy to people through the sweeter things in life.

We specialize in excellent taste and texture in all our bakery products, safe for those with Celiac Disease or who are gluten intolerant and sensitive. A healthy lifestyle choice for all.

Meet Chef Rue

Chef Rue

is a fourth-generation baker born into the business owned & operated by her parents. Countless hours were spent in the bakery where her Mother, Mrs Salani., a master baker, taught Chef Rue everything about baking and running a business. They dreamed that together, they would make Tantalize Bakes Bakery a household name.
